DIY 'do it yourself'

How to make your own iphone wallpaper.

One: Choosing a color scheme

First, choose a color scheme that you want to follow while creating the background.

Two: Find magazines and look through them

Next, gather up some magazines and use them to find images for the collage. Try to pick things that stick out to you or that follow your color scheme. Once you find something that stands out, cut it out however you want. I suggest cutting some images into squares and some images against the border of the object to give the collage variety.

Three: Creating collage

Using the collection of images you cut from the magazine, lay out all of the clippings in a way that makes sense to you. Once you have a layout you are happy with, use glue to paste the magazine clippings onto a thick piece of paper. Make sure you use something thick enough to handle the glue. Wait for it to dry

Four: taking a picture

After the glue has dred, the collage is finshed. Now, take a picture of the collage. You can crop and edit the photo to your liking, and set that picture as your background.
