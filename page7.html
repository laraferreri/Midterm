How to make a rainbow on your windsheild

One: Choose paint colors

Decide which colors you would like to incorrperate in the rainbow.

Two: Add drops of colors

Next, squeeze the paint out in circles next to eachother. Make sure you put the paint close enough together so there is not gaps in between the lines. Another thing to remember is the placement of the paint. Every car will be different but just put it in a place where the windsheild whipper can reach it.

Three: Paint the windsheild

Turn on the windheild to get a rainbow effect. Although this is very usless, it can still be fun and is great for pictures.
