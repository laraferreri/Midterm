How to make your own Scrunchie.

One: Cut and stitch a piece of fabric

Cut a piece of fabric 4" x 20" and fold the fabric in half. Stitch the long edge that was folded over. After this is done turn the fabric insdie out.

Two: Cut Elastic

Cut and thread a 9" long piece of elastic through the fabric. It could be helpful to use something to help thread it through. Once it is through tie the two ends of the elastic in a knot.

Three: Stitching Scrunchie Closed

Move the fabrric around the piece of elastic so that the knot is not in the front. Then overlap the ends of the fabric and stitch them together.
