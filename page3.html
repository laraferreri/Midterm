How to transform sweatpants into a trendy peice of clothing.

One: Choose a pair of sweatpants

When choosing your sweatpants, make sure they are a little bigger then your normal size becuase you will be cutting the sides. It is important to think about this becuase the shape you will cut is different depending on the style of the pant.

Two: Cut pant legs.

Cut the bottom of the pants in a traingle shape if the sweatpants have flare bottoms. If the pants have a cuffed bottom, cut a rectangular slit up the side-- about the width of a saftey pin.

Three: Add saftey pins

Next add bobby pins connecting the sweatpants where you made the insision. I used around 16-20 saftey pins for each leg.

Four: Add chains

Add a chain using a saftey pin so that it can be detached.
