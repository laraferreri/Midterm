How to make your own purse out of pop tabs.

One: Collect enough pop tabs

Having enough pop tabs is a crucial part to this project being succesful. Collect about 1000. You will also need metal cutters, a staple remover, and an old key.

Two: Bending tabs

Use the key to bend the inner part of the tab from the top side. Bend it all the way back so that the tab can lay flat on a surface.

Three: Cut the tab

Cut the middle of the tab on all of the tab. Make sure that you \ cut the same place on each tab.

Four: Bending tabs

Using the stapple remover, bend each tab by putting the cut side in first and close down. This will create a bend in the tab where the metal bridge is.

Five: Weaving tabs together

Push one of the tabs through the cut on another tab to create a link between them. After this, do the same thing on the opposite side. Connect the second and third tabs together by putting a tab through both of them. This will create a weave-like pattern. Keep doing this in a pattern until you get your desired look.
