How to make your own Bouncy Ball.

One: Make Borax Solution

Mix together 1/2 cup of warm water and 1 tablespoon of borax until its dissolved.

Two: Add glue

Add glue into the mixture from step one. The amount of glue added is in sync with how big the ball will be. Add different types and colors of glue to change the color.

Three: form the ball

Take the solution and roll it into a ball shape. Try to do this as soon as possible because it will begin to harden.
